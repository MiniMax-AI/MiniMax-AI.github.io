MiniMax TTS Tech Report

MiniMax Team May 2025
[Report]

Abstract

We introduce Minimax-Speech, an autoregressive Transformer-based text-to-speech (TTS) model thatgenerates semantic features, and convert them into high-quality audio using a flow matching mechanism.By leveraging an audio encoder to extract timbre features from a reference audio, Minimax-Speechcan produce highly expressive speech with timbre consistent with the reference in a zero-shot manner.Furthermore, it supports one-shot voice cloning, achieving exceptionally high similarity to the targetvoice. Perceptual audio quality of the synthesized audio is also notably high, enhanced through theuse of Flow-VAE. Our model supports dozens of languages and demonstrates excellent performanceacross multiple objective metrics and subjective evaluations. Another key strength of Minimax-Speechis its extensibility without modifying the base model, enabling various applications such as: arbitraryvoice emotion control via LoRA; text-driven timbre generation by synthesizing timbre features directlyfrom text; and Professional Voice Cloning (PVC) by fine-tuning timbre features with additional data. Westrongly encourage readers to visit https://github.com/MiniMax-AI for more examples.
